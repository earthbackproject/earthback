Two worlds. The one we're building. The one we'd have to rebuild.

In the Current World, you grow hemp and bamboo, form alliances, trade with neighboring communities, and prove that local materials can replace a broken global supply chain. Everything you build can be exported as real construction documents.

In After the Fall, the supply chains are gone. You salvage, you grow, you build — or you don't survive. The same skills. The same printer. The same specs toggle. But now the stakes are everything.

The skills are real. The certifications count. The communities you form become real Alliance partners.
