START HERE

One Family. One Home. One Seed.

A farmer plants hemp in spring. Four months later, the stalks are harvested and processed into hempcrete — a lightweight, fire-resistant, carbon-storing building material. A 3D printer arrives. In days, not months, monolithic walls rise from the foundation. A metal roof goes on — engineered to last over a century, with solar panels already integrated. The family moves in. The walls breathe. The home stores carbon. It will stand for 250 years.

THE EARTHBACK APPROACH

Grow It. Build It. Own It.

Hemp

Community-grown materials. 0–10 mile supply chain. Carbon-negative from soil to structure.

3D Printing

Layer-free monolithic construction. Homes in days. Fire-resistant. Precision engineered.

Metal Roofing

100+ year lifespan. Integrated solar. From Spengler Industries. Built to outlast generations.

None of this technology is required. Every home can be built with conventional tools. We teach manual building first because real resilience comes from people, not machines.
THE NETWORK

No One Builds Alone

The Earthback Alliance brings together farmers, builders, engineers, communities, investors, and educators. Three organizations — one mission.

Foundation

Grants, education, community leadership. Long-term vision meets local action.

Alliance

Partner coordination, supply chain, workforce development. Making scale possible.

Project

Technology, deployment, innovation. Taking the best ideas from everywhere.

And to connect everyone, we built a platform. The people building a better world, sharing what they know.
