1. Who we are

Earthback is a community platform for people doing green building, sustainable construction, food systems, off-grid energy, mutual aid, and related work. We are operated by Earthback Project and organized as a community governed by the Earthback Core Constitution.

By creating an account or using the site, you agree to these terms.

To participate fully, you create an account with a name and email address. You're responsible for keeping your account secure. Don't share your login link. If you think your account has been compromised, email us immediately.

You're responsible for making sure what you post is accurate, doesn't infringe someone else's rights, and doesn't violate these terms.

4. What we ask of you

Earthback is a community of practice — people doing real work. We expect members to:

  • Post honestly. Don't misrepresent your skills, projects, or affiliations.
  • Engage constructively. Debate is fine. Harassment, threats, and targeted abuse are not.
  • Respect the focus. This is a space for green building and related work — not general social media, not politics, not noise.
  • Honor commitments. When you commit to a project, follow through or communicate early if something changes.
  • Protect others' privacy. Don't share someone else's contact information or private messages without their consent.

The full community expectations are in our Safety & Trust guidelines.
